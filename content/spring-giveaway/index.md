**This is a celebratory 5 year anniversary of the Greenhouse Plant Giveaway!**

Mark your calendar for **Saturday, May 2 from 3p - 5p** in my Garden!

There's a deep value for me that regardless of the bigger world's distractions, there's a joyful community sharing experience that I can offer each Spring and Fall. We are so fortunate to live in such a robust neighborhood and full of loving kindness. I love that we know each other, speak with each other, and value each other's humanness. 

In that space, I'm so glad to offer another plant season to neighbors and friends.
