Plant Orphanage

Adopt your free greenhouse plants!

Please select the number of plants you want to adopt! Availability will update after each request. The dates provided are when the plants are ready to leave their greenhouse home.

All seeds from Seedsaver Exchange and are organic; click on name for more plant info.

Once you have submitted your request, I will contact you for pick-up or delivery details.
